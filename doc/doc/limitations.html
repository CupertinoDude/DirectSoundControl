 Known limitations :

These are the known limitations  :
  • This version is only tested on Creative Audigy (2 ZS) hardware with latest drivers. Other hardware may or may not work.
  • DSC will only work with games based on the DirectSound(3D) API. It will NOT work with games that are using

    • WinMM
    • OpenAL (e.g. UT2k3)
    • Kernel Streaming (e.g. Foobar2000)
    • ASIO (most professional sound applications)
    • etc.

    so don't ask me, why it won't work with this games.
